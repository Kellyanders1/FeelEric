Biography.

Eric NKURUNZIZA is photographer, and filmmaker based in Rwanda. With over a decade of experience working largely with nonprofit and humanitarian organizations in Rwanda and around Africa, delivering high-end visual stories through film and photography.

He has worked with well-known nonprofit organizations and media platforms, and his work is known for its cinematic style, strong emotions, and attention to detail.

Expertise

Editorial Photography, Documentary Filmmaking, Visual Strategy.
